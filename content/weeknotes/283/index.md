  * Over the weekend we went to Sheffield as a belated birthday trip, because I couldn't eat or drink anything on my actual birthday.

  * It was lovely. We treated ourselves to first class trains because it only cost £10 more per ticket, then ate a 10 course tasting menu which was pretty great.

  * On Saturday morning we popped to meet [Lee](https://leemoody.co.uk/) and family for coffee and it was _so_ lovely to see them. Also quite impromptu which I don't have enough of – more of this please.

  * {{< pin right >}}{{< image "cemetery.jpg" >}}{{< /pin >}}

    We did a lot of walking around, went to look at some catacombs and a snuff mill, lots of steps.

  * On Saturday evening we met up with Joe and Niamh who showed us the good pubs. We had a spice bag and a good number of pints.

    After they left we went to [Public](https://www.publicpublic.co.uk/) and had amazing cocktails and a pickleback. A nice weekend overall.
