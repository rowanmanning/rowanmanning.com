    Charlotte was in Birmingham on Saturday and I decided to spend some of my day trying to recreate a Sausage & Egg McMuffin. It went astoundingly well first time, I surprised myself, sage and thyme is what makes it taste right and it was _so close_ but also maybe better because the egg was pretty good.
  
    The only thing to improve would be the hash browns, I didn't squeeze enough liquid out so they were a tad soft in the middle. I have since bought some cheesecloth so I can do it properly next time.

  * I spent a lot of the rest of the weekend noodling about with [Easypanel](https://easypanel.io/) on my VPS (thanks [Kara](https://ghost.computer/), it's so great) and migrating some projects there. I also did some modernising of some of my open source libraries in preparation for dropping Node.js 20 support in April.
